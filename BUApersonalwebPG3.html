Sports was always very important to me growing up, from a young age I was in and around parents very involved in atheletics. My father play Division I football for the University of Maine and having him as my idol I wanted to do the same. Once I got to high school I realize I like football bit more than basketball so I pursued football instead. This led to many football camps throughout the summer and different combines to showcase my talent for different colleges. In the end I chose to attend the University of Maine like my Father. Today I am a freshman on the University of Maine's football team. It is a very large time commitment having to attend practices meetings and lives in between classes. Waking up at 5:30 AM or five days of the school week isn't the greatest, but it gets me up and ready to go for the day. My first year of college has almost come to an end it seems like it's gone by so fast. I have learned a lot and I can't wait to see what the future holds athletically and academically.
