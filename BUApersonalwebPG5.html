Hunter Smith: Wide Receiver

Here is my Bio from the 2016 season: Hunter was a redshirt this year lifting and practicing with the team but didn't dress for games he will have five years total in the football program. Moving on to next season we will be able to use him possibly for special-teams and some of the offenses areas. Hunter is from Dover-Foxcroft Maine where he played Highschool football and was a 4 year player for head coach Danny White's Foxcroft Academy Ponies. He played wide receiver his freshman and sophomore years and was switched to Quarterback his junior and senior years. After his senior season was ended from a left hip dislocation and he was back to 100% he played in the Blue-Grey All-American Bowl in Tampa, Florida at Raymond James Stadium home of the Buccaneers NFL Team.
